Our Story

Fresh Taste of Kenya

Bava Restaurant started with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of Kenya to every plate. Founded in 2015 by a passionate chef who believed that food should not only nourish but also tell a story.

We source our ingredients from local farmers, ensuring freshness and supporting our community. Every dish is prepared with love and care, using recipes passed down through generations.

From our famous Nyama Choma to our aromatic Pilau, every bite is a journey through Kenya's rich culinary heritage. We take pride in serving you meals that feel like home.
